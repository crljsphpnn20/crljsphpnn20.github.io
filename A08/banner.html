Main Characters

Jujutsu Kaisen follows the story of Yuji Itadori, Nobara Kugisaki, and Megumi Fushiguro, first year students at the Tokyo Jujutsu High. Yuji, after swallowing a cursed object, becomes the host of a powerful curse named Sukuna. Nobara brings fierce determination and combat prowess, blending her strength with a bold personality. Megumi, reserved yet incredibly skilled, uses his intellect and shadow-based techniques to face dangerous curses. Together, they confront dark forces and uncover deeper truths within the world of jujutsu sorcery.

"I don't know how I'll feel when I'm dead, but I don't want to regret the way I lived." - Yuji Itadori
