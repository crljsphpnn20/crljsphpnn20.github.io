  • Processor: AMD Ryzen™ Z1 Extreme (8C/16T, up to 5.10GHz)
  • Graphics: AMD Radeon™ RDNA 3 Integrated Graphics
  • Display: 7" FHD (1920x1080), 120Hz Touchscreen
  • Brightness: Up to 500 nits
  • Memory: 24GB LPDDR5 (Dual-Channel)

The ASUS ROG Ally X is a portable powerhouse designed for serious gamers and creative professionals. With a top-tier Ryzen™ Z1 Extreme processor, integrated RDNA 3 graphics, and a lightning-fast 1TB SSD, it handles demanding games and multitasking with ease. The vivid 7” Full HD 120Hz touchscreen enhances every visual experience, while the 24GB dual-channel RAM ensures applications run fluidly.

Enhanced with Wi-Fi 6E and versatile I/O ports, the Ally X offers high-speed connectivity and expandability. The upgraded 80Wh battery delivers all-day performance, and its refined ergonomics provide comfort for extended use. Whether docked or on the move, the ROG Ally X redefines what a handheld gaming device can be.
