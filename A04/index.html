Asus ROG Ally X

Experience next-level portable gaming with the ASUS ROG Ally X, a premium handheld PC powered by the cutting-edge AMD Ryzen™ Z1 Extreme processor and AMD Radeon™ RDNA 3 graphics. Designed for serious gamers and power users alike, the Ally X brings desktop-class performance to the palm of your hand.

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Processor: AMD Ryzen™ Z1 Extreme (8C/16T, up to 5.10GHz)
  • Graphics: AMD Radeon™ RDNA 3 Integrated Graphics
  • Display: 7" FHD (1920x1080), 120Hz Touchscreen
  • Brightness: Up to 500 nits
  • Memory: 24GB LPDDR5 (Dual-Channel)
  • Storage: 1TB PCIe Gen 4 SSD
  • Expandable Storage: microSD Card Slot
  • Operating System: Windows 11
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2
  • Ports: USB-C (x2), USB-A, 3.5mm audio jack
  • Audio: Stereo speakers with Hi-Res Audio
  • Battery Life: Up to 8 hours

Equipped with 24GB of high-speed LPDDR5 RAM and a lightning-fast 1TB PCIe SSD, multitasking, game loading, and file transfers are smoother than ever. Whether you're exploring open-world titles, emulating retro classics, or streaming your favorite content, the ROG Ally X delivers fluid, high-resolution gameplay anywhere you go. The immersive 7” Full HD display boasts a 120Hz refresh rate, vibrant color reproduction, and touchscreen capability—perfectly tuned for both fast-paced action and creative productivity. Enhanced thermals, ergonomic design upgrades, and long-lasting battery life make extended sessions comfortable and efficient. Whether docked, connected to an external GPU, or played solo on the go, the ROG Ally X is your gateway to premium portable gaming—uncompromised, unrestricted, and unforgettable.
